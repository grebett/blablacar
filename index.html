About me

Hello BlaBlaCar staff!

My name is Grégory Betton, I am a computer science student from 42 school and I've met you during your last afterwork.

I've been immediatly seduced by your spirit and your values and, after considering your job offers, I've decided to apply!

Although I could have given you a simple resume, I've prefered to build for you this quick website, not as a technical demo, but because it seemed to me to be the best informal way to explain you why we should work together.

I won't bother you with my personal details, as you may have already got them in the recruitment form. Instead of that, I do encourage you to visit the different part of the website using the left menu. If there is any question I haven't answered for now, let's keep in touch. I'll be very pleased to talk with you!
